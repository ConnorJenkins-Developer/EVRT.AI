Operating Principles

      
The rules we don’t break.

    

    

      

        1) Proof before scale
        
We pilot in days, validate with clear lift (CTR/CPA/ROAS), then scale. No hand-wavy “brand halo.”

      

      

        2) Guardrails over gut feel
        
Security and data hygiene are built-in. If a move risks trust, we automate the brake.

      

      

        3) Predict then decide
        
Predictive minute scheduling, mix modeling, and bandits make decisions obvious.

      

      

        4) Time to insight matters
        
Dashboards that surface winners in hours—not post-mortems weeks later.

      

      

        5) Build once, compound forever
        
Playbooks become software. Every engagement improves the engine.
